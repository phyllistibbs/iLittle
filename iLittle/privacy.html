Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Phyllis LLC (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the iLittle app (“the App”) collects and uses information. We value your trust and are dedicated to ensuring that your data is handled responsibly.

Information We Collect

iLittle collects anonymous analytics events that help us understand how users interact with the App. This information includes, for example:

  • Screen views
  • Button taps
  • Feature usage

These analytics events are:

  • Anonymous — no personal information is collected
  • Not linked to your identity or your device
  • Used only to help improve the App

iLittle does not collect or store:

  • Names or usernames
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Precise or coarse location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos, videos, or media
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data that can identify you personally

How We Use Analytics Data

Anonymous analytics data is used solely to:

  • Improve app reliability and performance
  • Understand feature usage and user behavior patterns
  • Prioritize future updates and enhancements

We do not use analytics data for advertising, tracking across apps, or profiling users.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share any user data. Analytics data may be processed by trusted third-party providers who act on our behalf, but all such data remains fully anonymous and cannot be used to identify any individual user.

Children’s Privacy

iLittle does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to access, delete, or manage.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us by mistake, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Your Rights

Because iLittle does not collect or store personal data, there is no personal information to view, modify, or delete. If you have any questions about data handling, feel free to reach out to us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect any revisions. Continued use of iLittle indicates your acceptance of any changes.
